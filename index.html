Welcome

Souls for Christ Ministries is a Christ-centered ministry devoted to evangelism, discipleship, prayer, and community transformation through the power of the Holy Spirit.

We welcome people from all walks of life to experience salvation, healing, restoration, and purpose in Jesus Christ.

About Us

Our ministry was founded with a divine mandate to reach the lost, teach the uncompromised Word of God, and raise spiritually mature believers.

We believe in the authority of Scripture, salvation through Jesus Christ alone, and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Mission & Vision

  • Mission: To win souls for Christ and disciple believers worldwide.
  • Vision: A transformed generation grounded in Christ.
  • Core Values: Faith, Prayer, Love, Integrity, Deliverance.
